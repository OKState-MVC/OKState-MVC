REFFING

In club-organized tournaments like the ones we play in, teams will normally only get a professional set of refs if they make it to the finals. In every other game, teams that aren't on will be assigned to ref. This means that every player of our team needs to know how to perform reffing duties, so that we can rotate people in and out of the job, and can do our off-court duties cleanly. You will be expected to study up on the videos detailed below, and be able to perform all responsibilities by the time of our first tournament.

There are a few different roles you will perform: line judging, up reffing, down reffing, scoring, libero tracking, and bookkeeping. That might sound like a lot, but they're all super chill and come really easily once you know how to do them.
