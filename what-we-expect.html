What We Expect of You Our Philosophy

Our goal with this Volleyball club is to build a culture of comradery. For everyone to be close on and off the court, so that when the time comes, you know that your teammate will have your back. We do compete, and we have an insatiable desire to win, but in order to do that, every single player needs to be dedicated both to continuous improvement, and team spirit.

There will be numerous off-court opportunities to get to know your teammates and find community; from cook-outs to Bible studies to dancing (my house hosts every Thursday), and much more. But it isn't all fun and games.

You will be expected to put in the effort if you want a spot. We have hired a coach, a personal trainer, and a nutritionist to give you all the tools you need for success; it's your job to take advantage of them. We expect you to study film diligently, practice with intention, take accountability for mistakes, and be an active piece of the team culture. No starting spots are guaranteed, and if you want to make varsity, you'll have to fight tooth and nail. But always remember that this club exists so that people have a way to athletically express themselves in college. Never lose your love for the sport, and never lose the connections you make.

We look forward to playing with you.
