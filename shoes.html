SHOES & EQUIPMENT

There are a ton of different options for equipment out there, and trying to sort through them all and find the stuff right for you can be quite an arduous process. That's why this page exists: listed below are a couple of great shoe options depending on your type of foot, some common sleeves/braces players wear, and an introduction to the most well-known volleyball apparel brand, Slunks.

These recommendations aren't supposed to be an end-all-be-all guide, but rather some suggestions to get you started.

SHOES

Nike React Hyperset A good all-around shoe; worn by almost everyone on the American Olympic Team
Nikola Jokić Joker 1 Great for larger players who need softer padding; our president's shoe of choice
Asics Sky Elite FF 3 Another great all-around shoe; worn by almost everyone on the Japanese Olympic Team
Giannis Immortality Series The best budget shoe on the market; ~$60 for a pair of pretty good shoes
D.O.N. Issue 6 My personal shoe of choice; incredible traction and padding, and very lightweight
Way of Wade Series The most popular Volleyball shoe brand; great traction and heel support on all models
Kobe 6 Protro Widely regarded as one of the best court shoes of all time; expensive, but top-tier

BRACES AND SLEEVES

Many players start with or develop joint pain and injuries over time, which requires them to wear extra gear to reduce athletic load (I wear knee sleeves, your president wears ankle braces, it's very common). Below is a list of popular gear to help you have fun without worrying about pain or injury:

Knee Sleeves Help reduce pressure on the knees, and allows for smoother diving. Jumplete & NEENCA are great brands
Ankle Braces Help provide structural support for your ankles, reducing rolling and spraining. Med Spec is a great brand
Shoulder Braces Help with rotator cuff injuries and hypermobility. Anaconda is the brand to look towards
Foam Rollers Great for warmups; helps blood flow wherever you use them. Amazon has a ton of these
Resistance Bands Great for training and warmups; help get blood flowing to shoulders, hips, and arms. Amazon has a ton of these
KT Tape KT Tape is great for bringing bloodflow to certain regions and alleviating pain for a while. Amazon has a ton of these

Slunks essentially has a monopoly on tuff volleyball apparel. It's honestly a little expensive, but if you wanna look cool on the court, you gotta be wearing slunks shorts. Their designs are awesome, and they come out with new stuff all the time. Our nationals jerseys will be made using this brand, and you'll see a lot of it at tournaments.
