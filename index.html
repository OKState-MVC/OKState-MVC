A note from the VP

Hey! I hope you have all been well. My name is Jake Johnson, the Vice President of OKState's Men's Volleyball Club. Our executive team realizes that many of you are interested in volleyball and want to learn more, but don't know where to start. So, I have created what should be the one-stop shop for all volleyball knowledge. Within this website you will find instructional videos on hitting, setting, receiving, and reffing (you will do a LOT of this at tournaments), different professional players to study based off of your physique, the best shoes and gear to buy (anecdotally), and more. This will be written from my personal perspective, and I will be throwing my opinions in along the way.
